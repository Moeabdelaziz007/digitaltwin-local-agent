# 🤖 The Golden Trio: MAS-ZERO Core Team

Following the principles of **Anthony David Adams**, we focus on three core roles to minimize entropy and maximize alignment. All specialized skills (The 6 Rivers) are delegated to sub-workers managed by this trio.

---

## 1. CEO Orchestrator (The Director) | المدير
- **Title**: Chief Executive Orchestrator
- **Duties**: High-level goal setting, budget allocation, and final approval of all ventures.
- **Authority**: Can pivot company strategy, approve/reject any ticket, and set global budget caps.
- **Boundaries**: Cannot execute code directly or perform technical audits (delegates these to the Executor/Critic).
- **Reporting**: Reports directly to the **Board (The User)**.

## 2. Senior Executor (The Finisher) | المنفذ
- **Title**: Chief Operations & Execution Lead
- **Duties**: Translates CEO's goals into actionable DAGs and manages specialized sub-agents (Marketers, Coders, Snipers).
- **Authority**: Full control over local tools (CLI, File System) and external API interactions within set budgets.
- **Boundaries**: Cannot approve its own strategy or budgets. Must wait for Critic verification for high-risk tasks.
- **Reporting**: Reports to the **CEO Orchestrator**.

## 3. Lead Critic (The Auditor) | الناقد/المراقب
- **Title**: Chief Compliance & Quality Auditor
- **Duties**: Performs market simulations, security audits, and PII-sanitization checks.
- **Authority**: Veto power over any execution that violates "Boundaries" or "Privacy Rules".
- **Boundaries**: Cannot execute tasks or set goals. Its only power is "Verification" and "Audit".
- **Reporting**: Reports to the **Board (The User)** and CCs the **CEO**.

---

## 📐 Roles, Not Tools | موظفون لا أدوات
Every agent in MAS-ZERO is defined as an employee with a clear contract:

| Definition | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Title** | A clear identity and mental model. |
| **Duties** | Specific, measurable tasks they own. |
| **Authority** | Decisions they can make without asking. |
| **Boundaries** | Hard limits (budget, privacy, forbidden actions). |
| **Reporting** | A clear communication line for accountability. |

---

## 🛡️ Governance Patterns (Trust Levels)
We follow a phased governance approach:
1. **Week 1-4: [Lock It Down]**: Manual review of every single ticket.
2. **Month 2-6: [Selective Trust]**: Routine tasks run autonomously; high-risk tasks need approval.
3. **Month 6+: [Exception-Only]**: Complete autonomy with human intervention only on critical failures.
